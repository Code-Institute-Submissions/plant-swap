1. Choose the plant that you would like to swap.

Once you have chosen a plant, go here and have a look to see if someone in your area is looking for it and if you like the plant they are offering. If so, you can contact them by selecting "Get in touch". If not, go to the next step.

2. Take a picture of your plant.

Get your plant ready for the spotlight and take some pictures. When ready, upload the image to a website such as https://imgbb.com/ and then open the "view link" of the uploaded image, right click on the image and copy the image address.

3. Upload your plant.

Go to Add Post and fill in the form. Make sure to use the url you copied earlier for the image. Once you submit, your plant should show up on the Posts page. Once it's there, you simply wait for someone to get in touch with you.

2. Get your new plant!

Once someone contacts you, you can sort out the details with them on how to swap. You can either send the plants to each other via post or meet up in person if you're nearby, that's completely up to you!
